PROGRAM DETAILS:

Fee per Credit: Tk. 4,500

ADMISSION REQUIREMENTS:
- 4-year Bachelor degree with minimum CGPA of 2.5 on a 4.0 scale
- Eligible disciplines: CSE, Computer Science, Computer Engineering, Software Engineering, ICE, or equivalent
- Engineering disciplines with calculus, statistics, and programming (prerequisites may be needed)
- Physical/mathematical/biological science with calculus, statistics, and programming (prerequisites may be needed)
- Must have passed HSC/Equivalent from Science group
- Must pass university admission test

PROGRAMS & COURSES FAQ
================================

Q: How many credits is the Master in CSE program?
A: The M.S. in CSE is 36 credits.

Q: What is the duration of the B.Sc. in CSE program?
A: It is a 4-year program requiring 140 credits.

Q: What are the admission requirements for M.S. in CSE?
A: A 4-year Bachelor degree in CSE/CS/CE/SE/ICE with minimum CGPA 2.50.

Q: Is there a thesis option in Masters?
A: Yes, students can choose between a Project (3 credits) or Thesis (9 credits) track.

Q: What core courses are required for Software Engineering major?
A: CSE412 (Software Engineering) and CSE430 (Testing & QA) are compulsory.
