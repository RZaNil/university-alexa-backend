CAREER COUNSELING CENTER (CCC)
================================

## Mission
To help establish links between prospective employers and East West University students and graduates, serving as a prime service provider in human resource and career development.

## Core Services
- Career Counseling: Individual advisory support
- Skills Training: CV writing, cover letters, corporate grooming, mock interviews
- Job Placement: On-campus recruitment, internships, vacancy announcements
- Job Fairs: Annual Fall and Winter Career Fairs
- Higher Study Guidance: Information on study abroad and scholarships

## Mental Well-Being (Socio-Psyche Counseling Center)
Professional counseling to support students managing stress, relationships, and emotional difficulties.
- Location: Room 306
- Hours: Sunday - Thursday (10:00 AM – 4:00 PM)
- Email: ccc@ewubd.edu

## Contact Information
- Website: https://ccc.ewubd.edu/
- Email: ccc@ewubd.edu
- Website: https://www.ewubd.edu/page/career-counseling-center

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================

Q: How many clubs are there at EWU?
A: EWU has 22+ co-curricular clubs managed by the Department of Student Welfare (DSW).

Q: What is the most popular club for CSE students?
A: The Computer Programming Club (EWUCoPC) and IEEE Student Branch are popular among CSE students.

Q: Does EWU have a cafeteria?
A: Yes, EWU has a modern cafeteria that can serve 500 people at once and prepare food for 10,000 daily.

Q: Is there a library at EWU?
A: Yes, the Dr. S. R. Lasker Library has 29,000+ print books, 100,000+ e-books, and 45,000+ e-journals.

Q: Does EWU provide transportation?
A: No, EWU does not provide a transportation system. Students use public transport.

Q: Is there a career counseling service?
A: Yes, the Career Counseling Center (CCC) provides career guidance, job placement, and skills training.

Q: Are there prayer rooms available?
A: Yes, dedicated Islamic prayer rooms are available, renovated in December 2024.

Q: What sports facilities are available?
A: Indoor games (table tennis, chess, carom) are available in common rooms. The Sports Club organizes tournaments.

Q: Is there mental health support?
A: Yes, the Socio-Psyche Counseling Center in Room 306 provides professional counseling (Sun-Thu, 10 AM - 4 PM).

Q: How can I join a club?
A: Visit the respective club's website or contact the Department of Student Welfare for membership information.
