SCHOLARSHIPS AND FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE AT EAST WEST UNIVERSITY

================================
QUICK SCHOLARSHIP LOOKUP (General Guide):
- CGPA 4.00: 100% Tuition Waiver (Medha Lalon / Top Performer)
- CGPA 3.90 to 3.99: Likely 100% Tuition Waiver (Top Performer Scholarship)
- CGPA 3.75 to 3.89: Tk. 55,000 - 60,000/year (Dean's List Scholarship)
- CGPA 3.50 to 3.74: Tk. 45,000 - 51,000/year (Medha Lalon Scholarship)
- CGPA 2.60 to 3.49: Eligible for General Financial Aid (Need-based)
================================

OVERVIEW:
East West University distributes around 9% of its total earnings (approximately 11 Crores annually) among 20% or more of its regular students. The university provides generous merit scholarships and need-based financial assistance to deserving students.

In 2023-2024, a total of Taka 22,87,61,468 (Twenty Two Crore Eighty Seven Lac Sixty One Thousand Four Hundred and Sixty Eight) was awarded in scholarships and financial assistance.

================================

1. MERIT SCHOLARSHIPS

A. TOP SCORER SCHOLARSHIPS (100% Tuition):
Full-year tuition waiver (for 1/4 of total credit requirements):
- Top scorers in undergraduate admission tests with minimum 75% marks:
  * 5 scholarships from Faculty of Business and Economics
  * 4 scholarships from Faculty of Sciences and Engineering
  * 2 scholarships from Faculty of Liberal Arts and Social Sciences
- Top scorers in graduate admission tests with minimum 75% marks:
  * 1 from MBA program (minimum 20 students enrolled)

Requirements:
- Minimum 75% marks in admission test
- Maintain minimum CGPA of 3.50
- Abide by University Code of Conduct

B. SSC & HSC A+ SCHOLARSHIP (100% Tuition - 4 years):
For candidates securing A+ in all subjects (including 4th subject) in most recent SSC & HSC examinations.

Requirements:
- A+ in all subjects in SSC & HSC
- Qualify in admission test
- Maintain minimum CGPA 3.50 each semester
- Register as regular student (minimum 9 credits/semester for undergraduate, 12 credits for B.Pharm and LL.B)
- Abide by University Code of Conduct

C. O-LEVEL & A-LEVEL SCHOLARSHIP (100% Tuition - 4 years):
For candidates securing:
- 7 A's in O-level (at one sitting)
- 3 A's in A-level (in one year)

Requirements: Same as SSC & HSC A+ Scholarship

D. DISTRICT SCHOLARSHIP (100% Tuition + Lodging - 4 years):
One poor and meritorious student from each district securing GPA 5.00 in most recent SSC and HSC examinations.

Requirements:
- GPA 5.00 in SSC & HSC
- Qualify in admission test
- Maintain minimum CGPA 3.00 (calculated based on last 3 consecutive semesters/one year)
- Abide by University Code of Conduct

E. SSC & HSC GPA 5.00 SCHOLARSHIP (50% Tuition - 1 year):
For candidates scoring GPA 5.00 (including 4th subject) in most recent SSC & HSC examinations.

Requirements:
- GPA 5.00 in SSC & HSC
- Qualify in admission test
- Maintain minimum CGPA 3.50 each semester
- Register as regular student (minimum 9 credits/semester)
- Abide by University Code of Conduct

F. O-LEVEL & A-LEVEL PARTIAL SCHOLARSHIP (50% Tuition - 1 year):
For candidates securing:
- 7 A's in O-level (at one sitting)
- 1 A and 2 B's in A-level (in one year)

Requirements: Same as SSC & HSC GPA 5.00 Scholarship

G. FOREIGN STUDENT SCHOLARSHIP (50% Tuition):
Foreign students admitted to EWU receive automatic 50% tuition waiver.

H. TOP PERFORMER SCHOLARSHIP (100% Tuition):
For top 10% students of each batch who have completed at least 1/4 of total credit requirements.

CGPA Requirements:
- Students admitted up to Fall 2025: CGPA 3.90 and above
- Students admitted Spring 2026 onwards: CGPA 3.95 and above

Credit Requirements for Scholarship (last one year):

Program | Credits Required
B.Sc. in CSE | 35 credits
B.Sc. in ICE | 35 credits
B.Sc. in EEE | 35 credits
B.Pharm | 39 credits
B.Sc. in Mathematics | 33 credits
B.Sc. in DSA | 33 credits
Information Studies | 30 credits

Continuation Requirements:
- Maintain minimum CGPA 3.50 (based on last one year)
- Register minimum required credits per semester
- Do not violate University Disciplinary Code

I. GRADUATE PROGRAM SCHOLARSHIPS (Admitted Spring 2024 onward):
- Bachelor CGPA 4.00 from EWU or reputed universities: 100% scholarship
- Bachelor CGPA 3.90-3.99 from EWU or reputed universities: 75% scholarship
- Bachelor CGPA 3.80-3.89 from other reputed universities: 50% scholarship

Requirements:
- Maintain minimum CGPA 3.50 each semester
- Register minimum 6 credits each semester
- Complete degree within stipulated time

================================

2. TRUSTEE'S SCHOLARSHIP

A Trustee member may award:
- 500% tuition fee waiver to five students (100% each) per semester
- OR distribute the 500% among more students
- OR maximum up to 45 credits tuition fee waiver per semester
- Unutilized credits can be carried forward within the same academic calendar year

================================

3. FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE

A. GENERAL FINANCIAL ASSISTANCE

Undergraduate Students:
Requirements:
- Completed at least 12 credits in immediate past semester
- Minimum CGPA of 2.70
- Demonstrated financial need
- Apply on prescribed forms at beginning of each semester

Graduate Students:
Requirements:
- Completed 6 credits in immediate past semester
- Minimum CGPA of 2.80
- Demonstrated financial need

B. FAMILY CONCESSION (50% Tuition Waiver)

When two siblings (sons/daughters of same parents) or husband-wife study simultaneously at EWU:
- Second sibling/spouse entitled to half-tuition fee waiver
- Both must be admitted full-time into regular programs
- Both must study within normal study time
- Benefit commences on admission of second sibling/spouse
- May extend to third sibling under same conditions

Requirements:
- One sibling/spouse maintains minimum CGPA 2.60
- Other maintains minimum passing CGPA (2.00 up to Summer 2023; 2.25 from Fall 2023 for Undergraduate; 2.60 for Graduate)
- First sibling/spouse not receiving any other merit scholarship/financial assistance

Note: Only ONE benefit (merit scholarship OR sibling/spouse benefit OR financial assistance) can be received at a time.

C. FREEDOM FIGHTERS' SCHOLARSHIP (Up to 100% Tuition Waiver)

EWU reserves 3% admission quota for wards of Freedom Fighters of all categories.

Maximum 100% tuition waiver for wards of:
- Wounded Freedom Fighters
- Deceased Freedom Fighters
- Financially distressed Freedom Fighters

Requirements:
- Qualify in EWU admission test
- Provide proof of parent being Freedom Fighter
- Maintain CGPA 2.60 or more each semester
- Register minimum required credits per semester
- Do not violate University Disciplinary Code

5. EWU EMPLOYEE BENEFITS

A. Children of EWU Employees:
- One child gets 50% tuition fee waiver during parent's entire tenure
- Subject to admission and financial assistance requirements
- Continuation subject to maintaining minimum passing CGPA

B. Employees Studying at EWU:
- 50% tuition fee waiver
- Maintain minimum passing CGPA of respective program
- Register for minimum course/credits each semester
- Benefit continues until employment at EWU continues

================================

IMPORTANT NOTES:

1. Scholarships/financial assistance available only within regular study time (e.g., 4 years for Bachelor's)
2. Students who completed required minimum total credits not eligible for scholarship/financial assistance
3. Students retaking courses not eligible for any scholarship
4. No student entitled to benefit from more than one scholarship/financial assistance scheme at any time
5. All scholarship benefits effective for minimum stipulated credits to complete degree within minimum stipulated time

APPLICATION PROCESS:
- Apply on prescribed forms at beginning of each semester
- Selection through computerized system ensuring transparency
- Processed by Financial Assistance Committee

Total Scholarship Distribution (Last 5 Years): Available upon request from university administration.

SCHOLARSHIP FAQ
================================

Q: What benefits do I get if I have Golden A+ in SSC and HSC?
A: You are eligible for 100% tuition waiver for 4 years if you have GPA 5.00 (excluding 4th subject) in both exams.

Q: Is there a scholarship for siblings?
A: Yes, there is a Sibling Benefit scholarship.

Q: Can I get a scholarship based on my semester results?
A: Yes, the Top Performer Scholarship (Merit Scholarship) is awarded to the top 10% students of each batch.

Q: What CGPA do I need to maintain my Merit Scholarship?
A: You typically need to maintain a CGPA of 3.50.

Q: Do I get financial aid if I am a Freedom Fighter's child?
A: Yes, there is a Freedom Fighter Quota scholarship.

Q: Can I apply for financial aid if I cannot afford tuition?
A: Yes, you can apply for Need-based Financial Aid.

Q: When should I apply for scholarships?
A: You must apply on prescribed forms at the beginning of each semester.
