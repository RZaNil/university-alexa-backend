## Dr. Md. Mozammel Huq Azad Khan
- Position: Professor
- Email: mhakhan@ewubd.edu

Academic Background:
- Ph.D in Computer Science and Engineering, Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET), Dhaka
- M.Sc Engg. in Computer Engineering, BUET, Dhaka
- B.Sc Engg. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering, BUET, Dhaka

Short Biography:
Dr. Md. Mozammel Huq Azad Khan is currently a Professor of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at East West University, Dhaka. He is one of the pioneering professors of Computer Science and Engineering in Bangladesh. He started his teaching career in 1986 and has held significant positions at RUET, Khulna University, and East West University, including founding departments and serving as Dean. His research focuses on reversible and quantum computing, among other areas.

Professional Experience:
- Professor, East West University (1999 - Present)
- Dean, Faculty of Sciences and Engineering, Khulna University
- Founder Head & Professor, CSE Department, Khulna University (1991 - 1999)
- Founder Head, ECE Department, Khulna University (1997)
- Lecturer/Professor, Rajshahi University of Engineering and Technology (1986 - 1991)

Research Interests:
- Reversible/Quantum Logic
- Multiple-Valued Logic
- Evolutionary Algorithms
- Graph Problems
- Data Mining
- Machine Learning

Selected Publications:
- "STICA: Spanning-Tree-Based Identification of Clusters With Agglomeration for Numerical Data," IEEE Access, 2024.
- "Rule-Based Synthesis of Ternary Reversible Up/Down Counters," IAENG Int. J. Computer Science, 2022.
- Digital Logic Design (Book), UGC of Bangladesh, 2006.
