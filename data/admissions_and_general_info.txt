EWU Admissions, Programs, and General Information

## ADMISSION INFORMATION

### Current Admission Deadline
**Spring 2026 Semester:**
- Application Deadline: December 04, 2025
- Admission Test Date: Sunday, December 07, 2025

### How to Apply
- Apply Online: https://admission.ewubd.edu/
- Application Fee: Tk. 600
- Payment Methods: Mobile banking (bKash) or cash at EWU Admission Office

### Required Documents for Admission
During online application, you must upload:
- Scanned passport-sized color photograph
- Scanned signature
- SSC/O-Level transcripts and certificates
- HSC/A-Level (or equivalent) transcripts and certificates

### International Student Admission
International students can apply with:
- 12 years of schooling
- Satisfactory grade equivalent (e.g., SAT score of 1100 or upper 50% marks)
- May be waived from written admission test

## SCHOLARSHIPS AND FINANCIAL AID

### Scholarship Categories
**100% Tuition Waiver:**
- For students with GPA 5.00 in both SSC & HSC (excluding 4th subject)
- Duration: 4 years

**50% Tuition Waiver:**
- For students with GPA 5.00 (including 4th subject)
- Duration: First year

**Merit Scholarship:**
- Based on high admission test scores
- For top scorers

**Other Scholarships:**
- Need-based Financial Aid
- Freedom Fighter Quota
- Sibling Benefit

### Scholarship Maintenance Requirements
To maintain merit scholarship, students must:
- Maintain CGPA of 3.50 or higher
- Register for minimum credits per semester (usually 9-12 credits)
- Have no disciplinary records

## CREDIT TRANSFER POLICY

### Transfer Eligibility
Credit transfer is possible subject to:
- Approval of Equivalence Committee/Department
- Credits from recognized universities
- Course grade of B/C+ or higher
- Syllabus matching EWU's curriculum
- Residency rules apply (minimum credits must be completed at EWU)

ADMISSIONS & GENERAL FAQ
================================

Q: What is the GPA requirement for undergraduate admission?
A: Minimum GPA 3.00 in both SSC and HSC. For Engineering, you need pass grades in Math & Physics.

Q: How much is the application fee?
A: The admission application fee is Tk. 600.

Q: Can I apply online?
A: Yes, apply online at https://admission.ewubd.edu/.

Q: What documents are needed for admission?
A: You need scanned photos, signature, and original SSC/HSC transcripts and certificates.

Q: What is the tuition fee for CSE?
A: The fee is Tk. 6,500 per credit. Total cost for 140 credits is approximately Tk. 10.03 Lac.

Q: Is there a waiver for Golden GPA 5.00?
A: Yes, students with GPA 5.00 in both SSC & HSC (excluding 4th subject) get 100% tuition waiver for 4 years.

Q: Where is East West University located?
A: Plot A/2, Jahurul Islam Avenue, Aftabnagar, Dhaka-1212.

Q: Does EWU have a dormitory?
A: No, EWU does not have on-campus dormitories. Students usually rent in Aftabnagar/Rampura.
